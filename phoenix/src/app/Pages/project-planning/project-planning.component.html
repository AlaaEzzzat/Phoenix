Consultants Healthcare Planning Services

Our healthcare planning services focus on providing support in the very early phases of planning during which an organization is determining the need and feasibility of selected healthcare services. PLSI-FZCO's objective in this phase is to assist in meeting the community's needs by helping determine a gap analysis, the appropriate size & services required.

PLSI-FZCO has been consulted for many turnkeyhospital development projects throughout the project life cycle (Vision - Invest & Funding - Design - Build - Equip - Licensing - Recruit & Integrate - Manage & Operate). This is further to conducting due diligence for many hospitals to improve their profitability, therefore transforming near to bankrupt hospitals to highly profitable healthcare operations through eliminating unnecessary redundancies then undertaking interim management to operate these hospitals during the change management & transformation process.
